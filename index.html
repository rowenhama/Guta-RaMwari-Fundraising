Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions concerning the construction of Guta RaMwari Centenary Museum

How do i contribute?

Contributions are done either with supplying of building materials/tools, financial assistance or donations.

What if I don't have the funds?

This is a great place to start, and also a great place to contribute. One can still do man power,cooks, cleaners etc

Who is eligable to contribute?

Every child of God is eligible to contribute

How often should i give my contributions?

We are encouraged to contribute as much as we can so that we accomplish our goals

Am I allowed to visit the site at any point of time?

There are time slots for visiting. Please contact your branch Evangelist for more information

What is the main purpose of building this museum?

The main purpose is to show the exhibition of the good works that were done by God and the historical monuments of Guta RaMwari, just to mention a few.
